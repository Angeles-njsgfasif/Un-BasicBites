Our Journey

"From a fun idea in our kitchen to a full-on teen cooking movement, The Un-Basic Bites has become a place where creativity and courage mix perfectly with cookie dough and frosting! Our very first video—‘Un-Basic Bites: Intro Video’—got the buzz going with 10 likes and supportive comments from day one. Then came the unforgettable Brookie Challenge with Caitlyn! Whether it was the full bake tutorial or the wild Roblox spinoff, our followers loved every second. One episode had over 6 likes and another hit 9, showing us that mixing brownies with gaming can actually work. But nothing hit home like Caitlyn's epic kitchen takeover. Between the Wilanne’s and Christman’s cuts, we received a combined 15+ likes! What drives us isn't just the numbers—it's knowing we’re helping teens find joy in creativity, confidence in the kitchen, and fun in every challenge. 100% likes across all our videos? That’s just the cherry on top. With 5 videos and over 51 likes combined, we're proud of every step."
